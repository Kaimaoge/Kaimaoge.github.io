About Me


I'm a Tenure-Track Professor, holding a position equivalent to a full professor, at the College of Computer Science at Sichuan University. My primary focus is on developing intelligent transportation solutions using data-driven approaches and machine learning. Before my current position, I worked as an IVADO postdoctoral fellow at McGill University. I completed my PhD at Beijing Institute of Technology. My research interests span various areas, including deep learning, spatiotemporal data analysis, and intelligent decision making and control. I have authored research papers that have garnered over 2000 citations on Google Scholar. Some of my work has been published in renowned scientific journals and conferences, where I served as the first author and corresponding author.

○ AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI, 2 papers)
○ IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering (TKDE, 1 paper)
○ IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems (TNNLS, 1 paper)
○ IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics (TII, 1 paper)
○ Transportation Research Part C: Emerging Technologies (TRC,2 papers)
○ IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems (TITS, 1 paper)
○ Applied Energy (1 paper)
○ Geographic Research Letters (GRL 1 paper)


Fortunately, I have also received several awards, including 2023 Tianfu Emei plan of Sichuan province, 2022 IEEE TII Outstanding Paper Award, IVADO Postdoc funding 2019 (by Institute for Data Valorisation (IVADO)) and Second Prize for Science & technology Development/Achievement of the Chinese Institute of Electronics.

I'm equally enthusiastic about applying my machine learning models to real-world applications. Some models have been successfully implemented within the TOCC platform of the Chengdu Municipal Transportation Bureau.
